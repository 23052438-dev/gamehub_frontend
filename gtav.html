Grand Theft Auto V

Grand Theft Auto V (GTA 5) is an action-adventure game developed by Rockstar North and released in 2013. Set in the fictional state of San Andreas, which is based on Southern California, the game follows three protagonists: Michael De Santa, a retired bank robber; Franklin Clinton, a street gangster; and Trevor Philips, a drug dealer. Players can switch between these characters to complete missions and explore the open world, which includes a variety of activities and side missions.
